How to Apply

Four simple steps to secure your child's place at Sunshine Sprouts Preschool.

  1. 1

    Enquire Online

    Fill the enquiry form on this page or WhatsApp us at +91 98400 12345. Our admissions team will respond within 24 hours.

  2. 2

    Campus Visit

    Schedule a visit to our Koramangala campus and meet the Principal. Campus tours are held every Saturday from 9:30 AM to 11:30 AM. Call us to book your slot.

  3. 3

    Submit Documents

    Bring the required documents (listed below on this page) to the campus to complete your child's registration.

  4. 4

    Confirmation

    Pay the registration fee and receive your official admission confirmation letter. Welcome to the Sunshine Sprouts family!
