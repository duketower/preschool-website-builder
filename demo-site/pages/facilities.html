Safe, Clean & Joyful Learning Spaces

Every corner of our Koramangala campus is designed with your child's safety, comfort, and joy in mind.

Safety & Security

Multiple layers of security so you can drop your child and leave with complete peace of mind.

At Sunshine Sprouts, your child's safety is our first commitment. Our Koramangala campus has multiple layers of security so every parent can drop their child and leave with complete peace of mind.

24 CCTV Cameras
0 Unauthorized entries since 2012
2 Trained Security Guards
  • 24-Camera CCTV System

    Monitoring all classrooms, corridors, the entrance, and the outdoor play area around the clock.

  • Electronic Gated Entry

    Only authorized adults listed in the child's profile are permitted to collect children from campus.

  • Visitor Log & ID Verification

    A visitor log book and ID verification process is maintained for all non-parent visitors entering the school.

  • Daily Attendance Notifications

    Attendance is marked at entry. Parents are notified via WhatsApp if their child is absent on any day.

  • First Aid in Every Classroom

    A fully stocked first aid kit is kept in every classroom. A trained first aider is on campus at all times.

Hygiene & Nutrition

A clean campus and wholesome meals ‚Äî because healthy children learn better.

RO-Purified Water

RO-purified water dispensers are placed throughout the campus so children always have access to clean drinking water.

Daily Sanitization

Classrooms are sanitized daily using child-safe, non-toxic cleaning agents approved for use around young children.

Clean Kitchen & Menu

Our clean, hygienic kitchen is run by a trained cook. Weekly menus are shared with parents in advance on our parent WhatsApp group.

Home-Style Fresh Meals

We serve a freshly cooked, home-style morning snack and mid-day meal. No packaged or processed food is served on campus.

Regular Health Check-Ups

We conduct regular health check-ups for all children. A paediatrician contact is available for emergency medical guidance.

Supervised Hygiene Routine

Separate, clean toilet facilities for boys and girls. Teachers supervise a hand-washing and hygiene routine after every meal.

Learning Spaces

Thoughtfully designed spaces that spark curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning.

Bright, Ventilated Classrooms

Our AC-equipped classrooms feature child-height furniture, Montessori material shelves, colourful learning walls, and generous natural light. We maintain a maximum ratio of 15 children per teacher to ensure individual attention for every child.

  • Air-conditioned with natural ventilation
  • Child-height furniture & Montessori shelves
  • Max 15 children per teacher
  • Colourful, stimulating learning walls

Library & Reading Corner

Our library holds 400+ age-appropriate books in English, Kannada, and Hindi. Children sink into cosy bean bags and reading mats to explore stories at their own pace. Weekly story-time sessions are a favourite with children and parents alike.

  • 400+ books in English, Kannada & Hindi
  • Cosy bean bags and reading mats
  • Weekly story-time sessions
  • Curated by age-group and interest

Dedicated Art Room

A purpose-built art room with fully washable surfaces lets children create without limits. Stocked with paints, clay, craft materials, and Montessori sensorial kits, it's where little imaginations run free every single day.

  • Fully washable surfaces
  • Paints, clay, and craft materials
  • Montessori sensorial kits
  • Dedicated art teacher
