What We Stand For

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted early childhood centre in Koramangala, where every child is seen, heard, and celebrated. We envision a community where young learners in Bengaluru grow into confident, compassionate, and curious individuals ready to embrace the world.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, joyful, and stimulating environment that nurtures a lifelong love for learning through play, exploration, and care. We partner with Bengaluru families to support the holistic development of every child ‚Äî cognitively, socially, emotionally, and physically.

How We Teach

Our Philosophy

At Sunshine Sprouts, we believe children learn best when they are happy, curious, and free to explore. Our approach blends Montessori principles ‚Äî where the child leads the pace ‚Äî with structured phonics and numeracy. We complement this with activity-based learning: drama, music, art, and movement.

Every classroom at our Koramangala campus is a prepared environment designed to invite discovery. Materials are accessible at child height, schedules are predictable but flexible, and every lesson is designed to feel like an adventure rather than a chore.

We deeply value the partnership between school and family. Our teachers communicate openly with parents, sharing daily updates, monthly reports, and honest conversations about each child's growth ‚Äî because when school and home work together, children truly thrive.

Our Core Pillars

  • Child-Led Learning Every child sets their own pace. We follow their curiosity and celebrate their unique way of growing.
  • Phonics & Early Literacy A structured, joyful phonics programme that builds reading and writing readiness from Nursery onwards.
  • Sensory & Motor Development Hands-on materials, outdoor play, and art nurture both fine and gross motor skills daily.
  • Social-Emotional Learning We teach children to name feelings, resolve conflicts kindly, and build genuine friendships.
  • Parent Partnership We view every parent as a co-educator. Open communication, regular updates, and collaborative goal-setting.
