How We Teach

Our Teaching Methodology

Every lesson at Sunshine Sprouts is shaped by research-backed methods tailored for the early childhood years ‚Äî so children learn deeply, joyfully, and at their own pace.

Montessori Principles

Child-led exploration using Montessori materials. Children choose their activities, work independently, and build concentration and intrinsic motivation naturally.

Structured Phonics

A systematic, multi-sensory phonics programme that builds sound-letter awareness, blending skills, and reading fluency from Nursery through UKG.

Activity-Based Learning

Drama, music, art projects, science experiments, and hands-on games make abstract concepts tangible and memorable for young learners.

Social-Emotional Skills

Dedicated circle time, conflict resolution practice, and emotion-naming activities build empathy, resilience, and healthy relationships from day one.

Bilingual Exposure

English-medium instruction with deliberate Kannada integration. Children learn to communicate confidently in both languages ‚Äî honouring our Bengaluru roots.

Regular Parent Feedback

Monthly progress reports, WhatsApp daily updates, and structured parent-teacher meetings keep every family actively involved in their child's learning journey.

Daily Schedule

A Day at Sunshine Sprouts

Every day is thoughtfully structured ‚Äî balancing learning, play, rest, and creativity so children thrive without ever feeling overwhelmed.

8:15 AM

Arrival & Free Play

Children arrive, settle in, and choose from open play stations ‚Äî blocks, puzzles, drawing, or the reading corner. A calm, welcoming start to the day.

8:30 AM

Morning Circle & Attendance

The whole class gathers for greetings, calendar time, weather check, and a morning song. Builds community, language skills, and classroom belonging.

9:00 AM

Structured Learning

Focused phonics, early maths, or language sessions led by the class teacher. Lessons are activity-based, interactive, and tailored to each program level.

10:00 AM

Snack Break & Hygiene Routine

Children wash hands, enjoy their home-packed snack, and learn hygiene habits ‚Äî table manners, waste disposal, and self-care in a warm, supervised setting.

10:20 AM

Activity Session

Rotating enrichment activities ‚Äî art and craft, science exploration, music and movement, dramatic play, or cooking without fire. The favourite part of every child's day.

11:15 AM

Outdoor Play & Physical Education

Supervised outdoor time in our shaded play area. Gross motor activities, yoga, free play, and structured PE games led by Mr. Rohan Nair.

12:00 PM

Story Time & Closing Circle

A calming read-aloud session followed by reflection, sharing, and a goodbye song. Children leave with a sense of accomplishment and something to share at home.

12:30 PM

Dismissal

Safe, organised dismissal with teacher handoff to parents or authorised guardians. WhatsApp activity update sent to parents after dismissal.
